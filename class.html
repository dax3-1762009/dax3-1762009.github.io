UW Medical Center

UW Medical Center has been designated an Aetna Institute of Quality for Cardiac Medical Intervention. This designation reflects the broad scope of services and excellent outcomes in every aspect of procedural cardiac care provided at UWMC through UW Medicine's Regional Heart Center.

UW Medicine hospitals and programs rank highly in U.S. News & World Report's annual "Best Hospitals" issue. Since 2012, UW Medical Center has been ranked No. 1 in the state and Seattle metro area by U.S. News & World Report. In the most current rankings for 2018-2019, UW Medical Center is ranked among the nation's top 20 hospitals
